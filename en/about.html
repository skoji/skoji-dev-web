About Guntosha

Gunto-sha(群島舎) is the trade name of Satoshi Kojima, a sole proprietor. Under Guntosha, I focus on projects that interest me, without worrying about making money for now.

Gunto means islands in Japanese. Kojima, my last name, means small island. Škoji means islands in Dalmatian.
